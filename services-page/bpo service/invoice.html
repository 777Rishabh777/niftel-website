Invoice data capture is a crucial part of invoice management. If you feel dissatisfied with the manner of your accounts payable data capture process, then we can be your trusted partner to efficiently handle this business process.

Our business process team can tailor the invoice capture process to meet your business needs. This will help your business not just in invoice data capture, but also in invoice data processing and automation.

  • Digitizing incoming vendor invoices (post or email).
  • Eliminate manual data entry and human error.
  • Indexing for quick search and retrieval.
  • Extract and process vendor information.
